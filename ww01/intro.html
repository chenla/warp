  • Ω — Omega Relative Density of the Universe
  • Λ — Lambda Cosmological Constant
  • Q Amplitude of Irregularities in the CMB

1.10 Graphs

Graphs treat the relationships between things as just as important as the information that they link to.

data
list, an random set of symbols and numbers.
information
key-value pair, a structured and labeled set of symbols and numbers.
knowledge
directed-graph, a map of relationships of information in a set.

insight
recognition of a correlation between two or more pieces of or classes of information and relationships.
